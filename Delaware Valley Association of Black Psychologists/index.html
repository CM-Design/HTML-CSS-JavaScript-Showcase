Delaware Valley Association of Black Psychologists

Serving Delaware, Philadelphia & South Jersey

Welcome to the Delaware Valley Association of Black Psychologists (DVABPsi). We are an organization of Black psychologists and professionals in related fields, who are committed to developing human resources within the Black community and interfacing with the community at large. We provide FREE monthly professional development seminars that are open to the public. These forums allow us to exchange ideas and information, review best practice and up-to-date research, and discuss practical solutions that reflect views on the causes, social problems, and issues that affect the quality and destiny of Black life.

In addition to professional development seminars, we also have a number of community events throughout the year such as our Kwanzaa Celebration, Career Day, and Jazz Brunch.
